## Terminology

### Workflows

The complete end-to-end logic that defines your agent's behavior and capabilities. Think of it as the agent's brain - organizing how it thinks and acts.

### Workflow Blocks

The fundamental units of work that make up a workflow. Each block represents a specific task or decision point in your agent's logic, working together to create sophisticated behaviors.
